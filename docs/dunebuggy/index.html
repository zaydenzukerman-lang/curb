The Story

Nine years cooking on yachts. Now cooking for you.

Before the truck, Grant Francisco spent nearly a decade as a chef on yachts in the Caribbean — where every plate had to be perfect, every time.

In 2022, Grant and Stephanie brought that standard home to West Michigan and put it on wheels: proper smash burgers, hand-cut flavors, and beach vibes wherever the truck parks.

Now Dune Buggy is one of Grand Rapids' most in-demand trucks — with events booking out months ahead.
