# Webblaze — New Client Onboarding Checklist

Send this as a friendly message/form the moment they pay. Nothing starts until the ★ items arrive.

## From the client — business basics
- ★ Business legal name + owner name
- ★ Phone number (the one that should ring) + email for leads
- ★ Services offered + prices (or "call for quote" per service)
- ★ Towns/areas served
- Hours, years in business
- License + insurance info (required if we're doing Google LSA)

## From the client — assets
- ★ 10–20 job photos, especially before/afters (phone pics are fine)
- Logo if they have one (we can clean it up: +$100)
- Any existing domain/site logins (Lead Gen Only clients)
- ★ Google Business Profile: add us as **Manager** (Settings → People → Add manager)

## We set up (our side)
- [ ] Site cloned from template, their branding/copy/photos
- [ ] Quote form wired to THEIR email (Web3Forms key)
- [ ] Click-to-call + text buttons with their number
- [ ] Domain purchased/connected (their name on registrar)
- [ ] CallRail account + tracked number (Growth & up)
- [ ] Citations pipeline started — Distribb (Growth & up)
- [ ] LSA verification kicked off DAY 1 (Growth & up) — owner does the Google screening; takes weeks, so start immediately
- [ ] Meta Business Manager access (Full Funnel only)
- [ ] Review link + QR + send-after-every-job templates delivered

## Kickoff questions (ask on the first call/text thread)
1. What job type do you want MORE of? (driveways? roofs? commercial?)
2. Who's the competitor that annoys you most locally?
3. Where do your current jobs come from? (word of mouth? FB? repeat?)
4. How do you quote — flat, per sqft, or on-site look?

## Payment terms recap
- Site: $1,200 one-time OR $500 setup with bundle · Lead Gen Only: $250 setup
- Lead gen: 3-month launch PREPAID at 20% off (Starter $1,080 / Growth $1,920 / Full Funnel $3,240)
- After launch: month-to-month at regular rate ($450/$800/$1,350), 30-day notice to cancel
- Ad spend: paid by client directly to Google/Meta, never through us
